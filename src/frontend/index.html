About llms.txt

llms.txt is a standard file that helps AI systems better understand your company, products, and services. Similar to robots.txt for search engines, llms.txt provides structured information for large language models.

By implementing llms.txt on your website, you can ensure that AI systems represent your company accurately and have access to the most relevant information about your offerings.
